About the Spa

SPA Melissa Allen is a relaxing day spa in the heart of Cincinnati’s Hyde Park neighborhood. We specialize in customized facials and gentle facial waxing. Open since 2014, we achieve the best results in skincare and beauty by incorporating advanced skin treatments such as HydraFacial, Microdermabrasion, Microcurrent, Ultrasound, and LED with high quality professional skin care products.

Our estheticians are professionally trained and licensed, and we will work with you to determine the perfect beauty and wellness routine for a healthier, more beautiful you.
